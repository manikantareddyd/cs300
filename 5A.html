''''Thomas Stoneman''' a.k.a '''Thomston'''<ref>http://www.coupdemainmagazine.com/interviews/interview-introducing-thomas-stoneman-aka-thomston</ref> is a [[London]] born, [[Newzealand]] artist from [[Auckland]]. He made his first EP '''School Night''' solely for his family and friends but later put the EP up on [[Soundcloud]], which was then quickly and unexpectedly picked up by international blogs such as [[Pigeons & Planes]]. Following up, Thomas then signed to [http://www.saikomanagement.com Saiko Management].<ref>http://www.windishagency.com/artists/thomston</ref>

Since then Thomston released [[Argonaut EP]], [[Backbone EP]] and a new single [[Expiry Date]].
